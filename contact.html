❤ Get in Touch ❤

🔒 Your privacy matters to me, and so does feedback!

I don't collect ANY of your personal data — by design. That means I have no way to contact you or receive feedback automatically. But I truly want to hear from you to make Warm Spirit better!

Since this is a static website (hosted on GitHub Pages), I can't provide a built-in anonymous comment system. Instead, I offer two simple ways for you to share your thoughts:

  • Requires GitHub login: If you have a GitHub account, feel free to log in and leave a comment in the box below. Your comment will appear as an Issue in my public repository.
  • No login required: Use the form further down on this page to send your feedback directly to my email inbox. No personal data is stored on my servers.

Your feedback means the world to me — thank you for helping shape Warm Spirit!
