Introducing: The Cocktail Memory Bar

My debut application is meticulously designed to help you preserve the vibe, flavor, and essence of your favorite drinks. It is a quiet space to document the moments that matter, far away from the noise of typical social networking.

Your privacy is my absolute priority. The app operates on a strict "Local First" architecture—meaning it runs completely offline. There are no servers collecting your data.

With zero external network requests or third-party tracking embedded in the software, every memory, photo, and tasting note you create is stored securely and entirely on your device.
