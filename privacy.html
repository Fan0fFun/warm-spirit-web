Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Warm Spirit, we believe your memories belong to you. This Privacy Policy applies to "The Cocktail Memory Bar" app.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. The App functions as a standalone tool on your device.

2. Where Data is Stored

All entries, photos, and notes you create are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account if enabled. We (the developers) have no access to your database.

3. Analytics

We do not use third-party tracking or analytics tools that identify individual users.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the contact form on this website.
